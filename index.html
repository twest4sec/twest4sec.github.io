Vision

I am seeking the position of Recording Secretary because I firmly believe my prior secretarial experience would make me a great candidate for the role. Serving as Secretary for other organizations, I have been responsible for composing bi-weekly membership reports, managing chapter membership roster, recording minutes for executive and general membership meetings, and handling correspondence .

As DAC continues to strive towards increasing our digital footprint in our community, our internal workflow must follow the same path. While continuting to build upon the position's current duties, I have a shared vision of streamlining our chapter’s documents by leveraging them to support:

  • Logistics Planning: Implement on-demand access to chapter communications using QR codes, automated text messaging, and email distribution to provide instant access to critical chapter information.
  • Committee
    Transition Planning:     Archive past committee records to streamline succession planning strategies for new committee chairs.
  • Financial Planning: Incorporate executive board and committee meeting minutes to support pre and post-budget planning.
I believe these factors would have a great impact on our chapter’s functionality and structure.
